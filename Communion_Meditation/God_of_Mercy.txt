God of MercyRicky Manalo CSP/Bob HurdRefrainGod of mercy, holy myst’ry,ever faithful one,you have pitched your tent among us,reconciling love.Heart and mind of Christ,live within our lives,form us into people of mercy and grace. 1. You have called us each by namein your great compassion,encompassing our failings with mercy.God of mercy, lead us.2. Slow to anger, rich in love,you bestow forgiveness.May we, in turn, forgive those who harm us.God of mercy, lead us.3. In our midst as one who serves,breaking down divisions,to all in need you send us as neighbor.God of mercy, lead us.4. Reaching out to those in pain,wounded and abandoned,may we create safe havens for healing.God of mercy, lead us.5. In the word of truth you speak,word of hope and challenge,you summon us to speak for the voiceless.God of mercy, lead us.6. In this Bread we break and share,in this Cup of blessing,you summon us to work for true justice.God of mercy, lead us.© 2015, Ricky Manalo, CSP and Bob Hurd. Published by OCP.
