[Verse 1]
Jesus, I trust in you in the long, dark night, 
when the shadows come and conceal the light.
Though my eyes can’t see, you reveal the truth 
to this heart of mine. Jesus I trust, 
Jesus, I trust in you.

[Verse 2]
Jesus, I trust in you when my cup is dry 
and I thirst for more than this world supplies. 
There is a well spring near you will lead me to, 
so I shall not want.  Jesus I trust, 
Jesus, I trust in you.

[Refrain]
Your mercy comes in a million waves, 
flowing through your heart to this world you made. 
In your life outpoured is my life renewed, 
Jesus I trust, 
Jesus I trust in you.

[Verse 3]
Jesus, I trust in you when I fear the path; 
though it’s rock and thorn, I will not turn back. 
Death cannot steal my hope; you will see me through. 
As you take my hand,  Jesus I trust,
Jesus, I trust in you.
