The Lord Is My Hope
M.D. Ridge

Refrain
The Lord is my hope and my glory.
The Lord is the song that I sing:
so tender and loving a shepherd,
so rooted in justice, a king.
When shadow confuses my vision,
when sorrow lays claim to my heart,
God is my refuge, my rock and my shield.
I will rely on the Lord.

1. Near to death, I cried, "Save me!"
and you heard.
You are God, and you
lift up the poor.

2. Kingdoms fall; nations tremble
at your pow'r.
None can stand without
you at their side.

3. Justice reigns, and the wicked
are cast down.
In your love is our
safety and strength.

4. All who trust in your promise
will be saved,
for your word has been
tested in fire.

5. Morning comes: I will praise you
with my life,
ever faithful and
true to your word.
