Litany of Peace
Barbara Bridge

1. God of love, 
your gift of peace is planted deep within our hearts: 

Refrain
Make me an instrument of your peace. 
Make me an instrument of your peace.

2. God of peace,wherever there is hatred, 
may we bring your love:

3. May we bring your hope and gladness 
where despair and sadness are:

4. May we seek to understand another 
with a patient heart:

5. Faithful God, wherever there is darkness, 
may we bring your light: 

6. God of mercy, help us to forgive 
when there is injury done: 

7. Gracious God, whenever people hunger, 
may we fill their need:

8. Set us free from all our fear and anger; 
set us free to love:

Text: Based on the prayer traditionally ascr. to St. Francis of Assisi,
