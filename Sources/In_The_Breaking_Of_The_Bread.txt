In the Breaking of the BreadTimothy R. SmithRefrainIn the breaking of the bread,we knew him, Lord Jesus.Alleluia! Alleluia!1. Were not our hearts burning as he spoke?Were not our hearts yearning for the Word of God?2. Put your hand here, see the place of nails.Blessed are those who have not seen, but believe.3. Risen is Christ, he who died for us. Risen is Christ, shepherd who died for his flock.4. Sing to the Lord, all you nations, praise.Great is our God, worthy to be praised.5. Taste and see, see and behold God.Taste and see, see your God and believe.6. I am the vine, you, the branches.Live in me, I in you, and bear much fruit.7. Is not the cup, Christ outpoured, our God?One bread, one cup show our oneness in God.Text: Based on Luke 24:13–35; John 15:1–2; John 20:19–31; 1 Corinthians 12:12–13.
