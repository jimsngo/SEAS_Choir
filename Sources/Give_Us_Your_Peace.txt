Give Us Your Peace/Danos Tu PazJesse Manibusan/Sarah HartRefrainGive us your peace, O God;give us your peace, your peace.Give us your peace, O God;give us your peace, your peace.1. When we’re afraid, running from truth,gather us back to you.Take every fear and fill our hearts.Give us your peace, O God.2. When we are tired, weary, and worn,take us into your arms,where there is no need, there is no want.Give us your peace, O God.3. Lord, we belong to you alone;show us the way back homeinto your light, into your love.Give us your peace, O God.EstribilloDanos tu paz, oh Dios;danos tu paz, tu paz.Danos tu paz, oh Dios;danos tu paz, tu paz.1. Cuando escapamos de la verdad,danos confianza, llámanos.Quita el temor del corazón:Danos tu paz, oh Dios.2. Cuando las cargas nos desanimen,entre tus brazos, llévanos.Nada nos falta si estás tú.Danos tu paz, oh Dios.3. Somos tus hijos, tú nuestro Dios,a tu morada guíanos,hacia tu luz, hacia tu amor.Danos tu paz, oh Dios.Text: English, Jesse Manibusan and Sarah Hart; Spanish tr., Iván Díaz and Juan J.
