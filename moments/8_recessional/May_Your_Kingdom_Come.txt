May Your Kingdom Come
Steve Angrisano, Sarah Hart, and Curtis Stephan/Vocal arr. by Rick Modlin
[Verse 1]
Spirit of God, fill us with fire, to shine your light for all those in need. Give us the strength to love in your name, so all the world will come to believe. 
[Refrain]
May your kingdom come, that justice may reign. May your kingdom come, that justice may reign. May our lives bear witness to the pow'r of your name. May your kingdom come, that justice may reign.
[Verse 2]
Giver of life, send out your Spirit; anoint your people that we might be healed. Send us with joy to the weak and the lowly, and let our love be your love revealed.
[Refrain]
[Verse 3]
We will go out proclaiming the kingdom; we will go out with mercy for all. We will go out and serve with abandon, with courage now to answer the call.
[Refrain]
