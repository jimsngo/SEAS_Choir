One Bread, One Body
John Foley SJ
[Refrain]
One bread, one body, one Lord of all, one cup of blessing which we bless. And we, though many,
throughout the earth, we are one body in this one Lord.
[Verse 1]
Gentile or Jew, servant or free, woman or man, no more.
[Refrain]
[Verse 2]
Many the gifts, many the works, one in the Lord of all.
[Refrain]
[Verse 3]
Grain for the fields, scattered and grown, gathered to one, for all.

Text: Based on 1 Corinthians 10:16–17; 12:4, 12–13, 20; Galatians 3:28;
