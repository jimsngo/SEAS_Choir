[Name]
Heart of My God

[Author]
Sarah Hart and Kelly Minter

[Verse 1]
Above my joys, beneath my sorrows, lives a peace greater than my need. Before the dawn, beyond tomorrow, love has been and evermore shall be. 

[Refrain]
Oh, the deep, deep love of Jesus, oh, the wonderful, merciful heart of my God. Oh, the wide, wide hope of my Savior; oh, the wonderful, beautiful heart of my God.
