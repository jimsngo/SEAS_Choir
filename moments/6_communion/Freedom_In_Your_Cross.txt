Freedom In Your Cross
Cooper Ray, Laura Ray, Tom Booth, Curtis Stephan, and Steve Angrisano; Vocal arr. by Scott Soper
[Verse 1]
Deep inside this hardened heart, amid a stormy sea, lies a love that aches for you and longs to be set free, to be set free.
[Verse 2]
You have come to ransom me, release me from these chains. To follow you, Lord, is my gain; I die to rise again.
[Refrain]
There is freedom in your cross, hope for all the lost. The bonds of our shame have fallen away. There is freedom, freedom in your cross.
[Verse 3]
Out of darkness, into light, my eyes now see your truth. I embrace this life you offer me and surrender all to you.
[Refrain]
There is freedom in your cross, hope for all the lost. The bonds of our shame have fallen away. There is freedom, freedom in your cross.
[Bridge]
Your love paid the price; what a great sacrifice. You freed me, you freed me. (Repeat)
You freed me Lord. There is freedom in your cross.
