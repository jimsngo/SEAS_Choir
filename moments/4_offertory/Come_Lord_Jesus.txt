Come Lord, Jesus
Steve Angrisano and Tom Tomaszek
[Refrain]
Come, Lord Jesus, come. Come and fill my heart with your life. Hold me close, Lord, hold me tight, and come, Lord Jesus, Come.
[Verse 1]
Where there's despair in life, Lord, let me be your voice of hope. Where there's injury, Lord, let me be your voice of peace.
[Refrain]
[Verse 2]
Where there is sadness let me be your comfort and your joy. When there's fear in our hearts let me be a sign of faith.
[Refrain]
[Outro]
Hold me close, Lord, hold me tight. Come, Lord Jesus, come.
