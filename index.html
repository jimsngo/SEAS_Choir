Turn your eyes, O God, our shield; And look on the face of your anointed one; One day within your courts Is better than a thousand elsewhere.

Taste and see the goodness of the Lord.
Owen Alstott

Whoever eats my flesh and drinks my blood remains in me and I in him, says the Lord.
Mass Of Renewal - Curtis Stephan
Offertory (Cederick & Joelle)
We Come to You
We come to you. We come to you broken, hungry. We come to you. we come to you. Make us whole again.

With the Lord there is mercy; In him is plentiful redemption.
Steve Angrisano, Sarah Hart, and Curtis Stephan

Miracle of Grace
Bread of life, bread of life. Those who eat this bread shall live and never die. Bread of life, bread of life. Your true presence in this holy sacrifice.
Curtis Stephan

Sacred Silence
Sacred silence, holy ocean, gentle water, washing over me; help me listen, Holy Spirit. Come and speak to me.
Tom Booth / Jenny Pixler / Anthony Kuner
Recessional (David)
High Above Our Way
High above our way is the mind of God, love much greater than my own. Into your hands I commend my life; true love is found in Christ alone.
Steve Angrisano, Curtis Stephan
