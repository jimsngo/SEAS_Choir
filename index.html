Nineteenth Sunday in Ordinary Time (B)

Sunday Aug 11, 2024 - 5:30 PM

Entrance Antiphon (Lorena)
Look to your covenant, O Lord, And forget not the life of your poor ones for ever. Arise, O God, and defend your cause, And forget not the cries of those who seek you.
Steve Angrisano, Sarah Hart, and Curtis Stephan
Responsorial Psalm (Lorena)
Taste and see the goodness of the Lord.
Owen Alstott
Gospel Acclamation (Lorena)
I am the living bread that came down from heaven, says the Lord; whoever eats this bread will live forever.
Mass Of Renewal - Curtis Stephan
Offertory
Drawn to You
Drawn to you, Lord, we are drawn to you, to the beauty of your presence in this place. Here for you, God, we are here for you as the gifts we bring become a feast of grace. We are drawn to you.
Sarah Hart
Communion Antiphon (Alan)
O Jerusalem, glorify the Lord, Who gives you your fill of finest wheat.
Steve Angrisano, Sarah Hart, and Curtis Stephan
Communion (David)
We Remember, We Believe
We remember, we believe in this bread of life, in this cup poured out, and together now receive the truth of Christ, in his saving love, we remember, we believe.
Steve Angrisano / Sarah Hart
Meditation (Alan)
Sacred Silence
Sacred silence, holy ocean, gentle water, washing over me; help me listen, Holy Spirit. Come and speak to me.
Tom Booth / Jenny Pixler / Anthony Kuner
Recessional (David)
Forever
Forever God is faithful! Forever God is strong! Forever God is with us! Forever!
Chris Tomlin
