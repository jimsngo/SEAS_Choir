Entrance Antiphon (Lorena)

May the Lord bless us all the days of our lives.
Owen Alstott

Beloved, Arise
My Love, Do Not Hide From Me; Show Me Your Face. Your Garden Enchants My Heart; Come Open The Gate. I’ll Walk You Through Fields Of White Till Sweet Morning Rise; My Shadows Will Take Their Leave Because You Are Mine.
Sarah Hart

The Lord is good to those who hope in him, To the soul that seeks him.
Let Us All Rejoice - Steve Angrisano, Sarah Hart, Curtis Stephan

Jesus, I trust In You
Jesus, I trust in you, giver of mercy. Jesus, I trust in you, O Font of Life. Healer, teacher, Savior and friend, Jesus, I trust in you. All that I am is in your hands.
Angus McDonell and Sarah Hart

For Your Glory
There is a time to live, There is a time to die. There is a time to laugh, There is a time to cry. There is a time to dance, A time for joy's embrace, And in all seasons, God we humbly seek Your face.
Matt Maher
