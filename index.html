Twenty-first Sunday in Ordinary Time (B)

Sunday Aug 25, 2024 - 5:30 PM

Entrance Antiphon (Lorena)
Turn your ear, O Lord, and answer me; save the servant who trusts in you, my God. Have mercy on me, O Lord, for I cry to you all the day long.
Steve Angrisano, Sarah Hart, and Curtis Stephan
Responsorial Psalm (Alan)
Taste and see the goodness of the Lord.
Owen Alstott
Gospel Acclamation (Alan)
Your words, Lord, are Spirit and life; you have the words of everlasting life.
Steve Angrisano
Offertory (All)
A Place at Your Table
Here in your presence the greatest are least, the burdened find rest and the hungry can feast. By love we’re invited, here mercy prevails. God, in your goodness, we share a place at your table, a place at your table.
Ben Walther
Communion Antiphon (Lorena)
Whoever eats my flesh and drinks my blood has eternal life, says the Lord, and I will raise him up on the last day.
Steve Angrisano, Sarah Hart, and Curtis Stephan
Communion (All)
Do This in Memory of Me
Take the living bread of life, Take the cup of sacrifice; Do this in memory of me. Take the body and the blood, Given freely out of love; Do this is memory of me; Do this in memory of me.
Chris Muglia/Scott Soper
Meditation (Alan/Lorena)
Sacred Silence
Sacred silence, holy ocean, gentle water, washing over me; help me listen, Holy Spirit. Come and speak to me.
Tom Booth / Jenny Pixler / Anthony Kuner
Recessional (All)
With One Voice
With one voice we'll pass the Word along; with one voice, bring justice to the world. And with all the angels we'll spread the goodness of God. With all power and glory the Word of God shall reign.
Ricky Manalo CSP
