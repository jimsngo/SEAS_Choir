SEAS Choir - Ontario CA

Twenty-sixth Sunday in Ordinary Time (B)

Sunday Sept 29, 2024 - 5:30 PM

Entrance Antiphon (Alan)
All that you have done to us, O Lord, You have done with true judgment, For we have sinned against you And not obeyed your commandments. But give glory to your name And deal with us according to the bounty of your mercy.
Let Us All Rejoice - Steve Angrisano, Sarah Hart, Curtis Stephan
Responsorial Psalm (Lorena)
The precepts of the Lord give joy to the heart.
Owen Alstott
Gospel Acclamation (David)
Your word, O Lord, is truth; consecrate us in the truth.
Mass of Restoration - Joshua Blakesley
Offertory
Jesus, I Trust in You (Cederick)
Jesus, I trust in you, giver of mercy. Jesus, I trust in you, O Font of Life. Healer, teacher, Savior and friend, Jesus, I trust in you. All that I am is in your hands.
Agnus McDonell and Sarah Hart
Communion Antiphon (Lorena)
Remember your word to your servant, O Lord, By which you have given me hope. This is my comfort when I am brought low.
Let Us All Rejoice - Steve Angrisano, Sarah Hart, Curtis Stephan
Communion (Cederick)
Do This in Memory of Me
I am the bread of life; All who believe in me will have eternal life. I am the bread of life; Come down from heaven So that all who eat this bread Will never die.
Chris Muglia/Scott Soper
Meditation (Cederick)
Hold On to Love
There is a place for the sadness. Hold on to Love. There is a season of gladness. Hold on to Love. When pain and confusion seem endless, hold on to Love. We cultivate healing through kindness. Hold on to Love.
Jesse Manibusan
Recessional (David)
For Your Glory
There is a time to live, There is a time to die. There is a time to laugh, There is a time to cry. There is a time to dance, A time for joy's embrace, And in all seasons, God we humbly seek Your face.
Matt Maher
