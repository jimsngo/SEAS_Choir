SEAS Choir - Ontario CA

Eleventh Sunday in Ordinary Time (B)

June 16, 2024 - 5:30 PM

Entrance Antiphon (Alan)
O Lord, hear my voice, For I have called to you; be my help. Do not abandon or forsake me, O God, my Savior!
Responsorial Psalm (Lorena)
Lord, it is good to give thanks to you.
Gospel Acclamation (Lorena)
The seed is the word of God, Christ is the sower. All who come to him will live forever.
Offertory
Drawn to You
Drawn to you, Lord, we are drawn to you, to the beauty of your presence in this place. Here for you, God, we are here for you as the gifts we bring become a feast of grace. We are drawn to you.
Sarah Hart.
Communion 1
One Bread, One Body
One bread, one body, one Lord of all, One cup of blessing which we bless. And we, though many, throughout the earth, We are one body in this one Lord.
John B. Foley
Communion 2
Like The Bread
Like the bread, we are taken. Like the Christ, we are blessed. On this altar we are broken Giv’n as food that all might live, Giv’n as food that all might live.
Tom Booth
Recessional
Your Grace is Enough
For Your grace is enough! Your grace is enough! Your grace is enough for me!
Matt Maher
