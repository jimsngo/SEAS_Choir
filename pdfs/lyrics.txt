=== Communion ===

The Body of Christ
Sarah Hart
[Verse 1]
This is the Body of Christ, beautiful, broken and blessed; miracle of the greatest of loves, presence of God in our midst. This is the Cup that he shares, endlessly emptied and poured; sacrifice made so that we might be saved now and forevermore.
[Refrain]
Amen, amen! We are healed by the Bread of life. Amen, amen! We are one in the Body of Christ. We are one in the Body of Christ. 
[Verse 2] This is the Body of Christ, word become flesh by his grace; miracle of a myst’ry no tongue ever could fully explain. This is the Blood of the Lamb, gift of a heart open wide; poured from the Cross to the altar where love comforts and fills and provides. 
[Refrain]
[Verse 3]
This is the Body of Christ, gathered in hope and in faith; bound by this love, this Body and Blood he offers again and again. All of the hunger we have, satisfied only by this; so we receive, we remember, believe and share so that others may live.

=== Meditation ===

The Beautitudes
Francesca LaRosa
[Verse 1]
Blessed are the poor in spirit, for theirs is the Kingdom of Heaven. Blessed are those who mourn for they will be comforted. Blessed are the meek, for they will inherit the earth. Blessed are those who hunger and thirst for righteousness, for they will be filled.
[Refrain]
Oh, rejoice and be glad, for your reward is great in heaven. Oh, rejoice and be glad, for your reward is great in heaven.
[Verse 2]
Blessed are the merciful, for they will receive mercy. Blessed are the pure in heart, for they will see God. Blessed are the peacemakers, for they will be called children of God. Blessed are those persecuted for righteousness, the kingdom of heaven is theirs.
[Refrain]
[Verse 3]
Blessed are you when people revile you and persecute you and utter eel things against you falsely all because of me, in the same way they did to the prophets who were before you.
[Refrain]

=== Recessional ===

May Your Kingdom Come
Steve Angrisano, Sarah Hart, and Curtis Stephan/Vocal arr. by Rick Modlin
[Verse 1]
Spirit of God, fill us with fire, to shine your light for all those in need. Give us the strength to love in your name, so all the world will come to believe. 
[Refrain]
May your kingdom come, that justice may reign. May your kingdom come, that justice may reign. May our lives bear witness to the pow'r of your name. May your kingdom come, that justice may reign.
[Verse 2]
Giver of life, send out your Spirit; anoint your people that we might be healed. Send us with joy to the weak and the lowly, and let our love be your love revealed.
[Refrain]
[Verse 3]
We will go out proclaiming the kingdom; we will go out with mercy for all. We will go out and serve with abandon, with courage now to answer the call.
[Refrain]
