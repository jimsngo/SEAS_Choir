You Stand Knocking
Tom Booth

[Verse 1]
You spoke my name, and took my hand,
And called me friend.
Such faithful love, so undeserved,
A heart transformed.
Though I wandered and have strayed,
Still your mercy reigns.

[Refrain]
You were broken for me, arms wide open on a tree.
You stand knocking at the door,
Offering all I'm hoping for.

[Verse 2]
To those in dark the Lord cries out,
"Come and be saved."
Along the way we shall find rest
The Lord is good.
For God comforts well his own,
We are his alone.

[Refrain (2 times)]
