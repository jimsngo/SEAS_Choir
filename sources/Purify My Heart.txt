Purify My Heart

[Verse 1]
Purify my heart
Let me be as gold
and precious silver.
Purify  my heart
Let me be as gold,
pure gold.

set apart for you, my master, 
ready to do your will.

and make me holy.
Purify my heart
Cleanse me from my sin, 
deep within.
