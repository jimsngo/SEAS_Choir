From the Wounds of ChristRufino Zaragoza OFMRefrainFrom the wounds of Christ flows glory.From the side of Christ flows blessing.From compassion nailed upon a cross,may we become what we gaze upon.Communion Verses1. Mem’ry holds that meal of love;Jesus gathered with friends, sharing bread and wine;a new covenant was given, before he died.2. God revealed in simple food;See the crucified one, see the humble Christ,present now as bread and wine, that you may live.3. Come be fed, come forth in awe.Here the Body and Blood of our saving Lordmakes us one with Jesus broken, and one with all.Good Friday Verses1. Turn our lives to face the woodof his sorrow and pain and his humble death.Through this cross God brought new life to the world.2. Firm as ground this wood we hold,as a ladder of hope, staircase of God’s will.Teach us mercy, love and justice; Holy Cross.

3. Come and touch the saving crosshidden from our lives in the outcast poor;in their struggle we will find God’s own face.Text inspired by The Second Letter of St. Clare (of Assisi) to St. Agnes of Prague, ca. 1237.
