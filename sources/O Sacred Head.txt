O Sacred HeadBob Hurd1. O Sacred Head, surroundedby crown of piercing thorn!O bleeding Head, so wounded,reviled and put to scorn!2. No comeliness or beautyyour wounded face betrays.Yet angel hosts adore youand tremble as they gaze.3. My sister rendered voiceless, demeaned and still in chains,my brother still exploited, images of your pain.4. Life-giving love, empow’r uswith courage bold and trueto walk the road of justice,and bear the cross with you,and bear the cross with you.Text: Verses 1 & 2 ascr. to Bernard of Clairvaux, 1091–1153, alt.; Verses 3 & 4, Bob Hurd.
