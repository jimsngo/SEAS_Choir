[Verse 1]
You take the old and you make it new,
And restore the hearts of the wayward ones.
You take the simple and make it beautiful.
Breathe within.

[Verse 2]
You take what’s broken and make it whole again;
You take the sinful, you make it pure.
Praise for the mercy you give so freely.
Breathe within, breathe within.

[Refrain]
O Blessed Light, within us shine;
You’re so wonderful, so wonderful!
O Welcome Guest, come give us rest;
You’re so wonderful! 
Oh, the wonders of your love!
Oh, the wonders of your love!

[Bridge]
Melt the frozen, warm the chill;
Cleanse this stubborn heart and will.
Make my life a song of praise,
Every moment, all my days.

[Repeat Refrain]
