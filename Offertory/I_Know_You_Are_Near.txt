I Know You Are Near
Cooper Ray

[Refrain]
I know you are near
When the rains come pouring down.
I know you are near
When the waves come crashing in.

[Verse 1]
My heart is steadfast,
Oh, my heart is strong;
for you, O Lord, are the center.
Your love reaches the heavens,
Your faithfulness to the sky.

You delivered me, Lord;
You lifted me up.

You alone are my rock,
You are my strength,
You alone are my refuge.
