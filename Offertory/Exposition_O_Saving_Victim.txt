Exposition/O Saving VictimCurtis Stephan/Steve Angrisano1. O King Exalted, savior of nations,we lift our eyes to heaven’s throne.Hear us, Redeemer, show us your mercy;we place our trust in you alone.RefrainO Saving Victim, open widethe gate of heaven to us below.Our foes press on from every side;thine aid supply, thy strength bestow.2. Might of the Father, way of salvation,no sin is hidden from your sight.Show us your mercy, free us from darkness,reveal to us your glorious light.
Bridge
Worthy is the Lamb.Verses text based on Attende, Domine; Latin, 10th cent.; Refrain text based on O Salutaris; St. Thomas Aquinas, 1227–1274; tr. by Edward Caswall, 1814–1878, alt. and adapt.
