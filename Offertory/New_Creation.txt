New Creation
Jackie François

Refrain
From death to life:
I no longer live,
but Christ now lives in me.
Clothed in righteousness
and crowned with holiness,
we are new. Alleluia!

1. See: the old is dead and gone.
Behold: new things have come.
Fit us for salvation.
Alleluia, alleluia.
Delight in redemption’s song.

2. O Lord Jesus, wash away
all of sin and death’s decay.
Make us new creations.
Alleluia, alleluia.
Delight in redemption’s song.
