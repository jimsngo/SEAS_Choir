[Title]
Litany of the Saints

[Author]
John D. Becker

[Refrain]
Lord, have mercy. Lord, have mercy. 
Christ, have mercy. Christ, have mercy.
Lord, have mercy. Lord, have mercy. 

[Verse 1-4]
(Saint Invocation), 
pray for us. (sing 8 times)
All you holy men and women, pray for us.

[Verse 5]
(Invocations: Lord, be merciful,...) 
Lord, deliver us, we pray. (sing 8 times)
All you holy men and women, pray for us.

[Verse 6]
(Invocations: Be merciful to us sinners,...) 
Lord, we ask you, hear our prayer. (sing 4 times)
Christ, hear us. Christ, graciously hear us.
