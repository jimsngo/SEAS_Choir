[Title]
Give Us Your Peace

[Author]Jesse Manibusan/Sarah Hart[Refrain]Give us your peace, O God;give us your peace, your peace.Give us your peace, O God;give us your peace, your peace.[Verse 1]
When we’re afraid, running from truth,gather us back to you.Take every fear and fill our hearts.Give us your peace, O God.[Verse 2]
When we are tired, weary, and worn,take us into your arms,where there is no need, there is no want.Give us your peace, O God.[Verse 3]
Lord, we belong to you alone;show us the way back homeinto your light, into your love.Give us your peace, O God.
