[Title]
Into the Day

[Author]
Suno/Jim Ngo


[Verse 1]
The stone is heavy, the air is still.
We wait in shadows against our will.
But hear the whisper, the holy breath,
That speaks a promise in the face of death.

[Pre-Chorus]
The grave is open, the light breaks through.
The old is passing, the world is new.

[Chorus]
Into the day! Into the day!
The chains of the silence are falling away.
Into the day! Into the day!
The love of the Lord is leading the way.
Unbind the spirit, let the heart go free.
Into the day! Our victory!

[Verse 2]
Go to the broken, go to the poor.
Open the window and widen the door.
Where there is weeping, let mercy rise,
Reflecting the glory in each other's eyes.
