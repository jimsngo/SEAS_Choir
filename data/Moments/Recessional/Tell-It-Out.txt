[Title]
Tell It Out

[Author]
Timothy Smith

[Refrain - X2]
Tell it out, tell it out, let the word be spread.
Jesus rose to save our souls, he's risen from the dead.

[Verse 1]
There's no misunderstanding, it's simple and it's true.
God sent us His only son, he died for you.
Without him we are strangers, orphans all alone.
Adopted by his sacrifice, we have a home.

[Verse 2]
Faith finds a way of knowing, feels a touch without a hand.
And the seed he's sowing guides our footprints in the sand.
But faith alone won't satisfy the hunger and the call:
Feed the hungry, heal the sick, somebody help me, when I fall.

[Bridge]
There's no misunderstanding, it's simple and it's true.
God sent His only Son, he died for you.
