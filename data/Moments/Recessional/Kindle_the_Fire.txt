[Title]
Kindle the Fire

[Author]
Jim Ngo/Suno

[Intro]
Come, Holy Spirit, come
(Kindle the fire of love)

[Verse 1]
O Light that shines from above
Glow within this heart of mine
Word of God, remove the dark
With Your majesty divine

[Refrain]
Come, Holy Spirit, come
(Fill the hearts of Your own)
Kindle in them now the fire
(Where Your great love is shown)
Come, Holy Spirit, come
(Fill the hearts of Your own)
Kindle in them now the fire
(Making Your mercy known)

[Verse 2]
Lord of life, as day recedes
Thanks we give for all our years
Through the labor and the toil
Through the laughter and the tears

[Refrain]
Come, Holy Spirit, come
(Fill the hearts of Your own)
Kindle in them now the fire
(Where Your great love is shown)
Come, Holy Spirit, come
(Fill the hearts of Your own)
Kindle in them now the fire
(Making Your mercy known)

[Bridge]
Breathe Your life into our song,
Where the broken are made strong.
Send Your wind and fill this space,
As we stand within Your grace.

(Fill the hearts of Your own)

[Outro]
