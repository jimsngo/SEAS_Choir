
[Title]
Tell The Good News

[Author]
SUNO xJim Ngo

[Verse 1]
With open hands and open hearts
We share the light that Christ has shown
His word of hope for all the world
Let every soul proclaim his love.

[Chorus]
Alleluia, alleluia, alleluia, Tell the good news.

[Verse 2]
Let every heart awaken joy
Let every creature praise his name
Let every land the gospel know
Go forth in peace let love proclaim.

[Chorus]
Alleluia, alleluia, alleluia, Tell the good news.

[Bridge]
Tell the good news let it ring
Hope and joy in everything
Shine the light let love renew
Hearts rejoicing strong and true.

[Chorus]
Alleluia, alleluia, alleluia, Tell the good news.
