[Title]
Great Will Be The Future

[Author]
Chris Muglia/David Brinker

[Verse 1]
We stand in faith declaring aloud the majesty of God,
His mighty power. 
Together we witness to all that He's done, 
and all that He's promised to bring. 
Together we lift up our voices as one, 
Together we sing.

[Refrain]
Greater will be tomorrow than today,
The future of this place.
Greater will be his glory. 
Come, discover now in Faith your destiny awaits.
Greater will be the future of this place.

[Verse 2]
We stand in faith believing our God.
He makes all things new in light and love.
Together we gather to lift up our hearts.
Glory and honor and praise, 
to the giver of life and the giver of love, 
the giver of every new day.
