[Title]
The Goodness of the Lord

[Author]Scott Soper[Refrain]I believe, I believe, I shall see the goodness of the Lord in the land, in the land of the living.[Verse 1]
The Lord is my light,the Lord is my rock, my salvation.The Lord is my refuge, guarding my life;of whom should I be afraid?[Verse 2]
Only one thing I ask:may I live in the house of my Lord.I shall gaze on God’s goodness all of my days; I shall live in the shelter of God.[Verse 3]
I believe I shall see the goodness of Godin the land of the living. Be strong, wait for the Lord.
