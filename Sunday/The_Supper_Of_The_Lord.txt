The Supper of the Lord
Laurence Rosania

[R] Precious body, precious blood,
seen as bread and wine;
here the Lord prepares the feast divine.
Bread of love is broken now,
cup of life is poured:
come, share the supper of the Lord.

[V1] This is the bread of God
coming down from heav'n,
giving life to us,
to all the world.

[V2] "I am the living spring
of eternal life;
you that drink from me
shall not thirst again."

[V3] "I am the bread of heav'n
giving life to you;
you that eat this bread
shall never die."

[V4] "All those who feed on me
have their life in me,
as I have my life 
in the living God."

[V5] All praise to you, O Christ,
present in this feast;
in this bread, we share
in one life, one Lord.
