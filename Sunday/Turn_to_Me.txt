Turn to Me
John Foley SJ

[R] Turn to me, O turn, and be saved,
says the Lord, for I am God;
there is no other, none beside me.
I call your name.

[V1] I am God, who comforts you;
who are you to be afraid of flesh that fades,
is made like the grass of the field,
soon to wither.

[V2] Listen to me, my people;
give ear to me, my nation:
a law will go forth from me,
and my justice for a light to the people.

[V3] Lift up your eyes to the heavens,
and look at the earth down below.
The heavens will vanish like smoke,
and the earth will wear out like a garment.

Text: Based on Isaiah 45:22–23; 51:12, 4, 6. Text and music © 1975, John B.
