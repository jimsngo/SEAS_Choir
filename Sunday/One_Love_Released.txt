One Love Released
Bob Frenzel / Kevin Keil

[R] One bread, one body, one cup, one call,
one faith, one Spirit present in us all.
One prayer, one blessing, one hope, one peace,
one church, one people, one love released.

[V1] Is not this bread we share, the body of our Lord?
Is not this wine we drink, the blood of Christ outpoured?

[V2] I am the bread of life, eat and you shall live.
To those who share this meal, my strength I'll always give.

[V3] I am the living bread, as manna from the sky.
This bread I give to you, that you may never die.

[V4] No one will come to me, unless our God has led.
And I shall raise them up, raise them from the dead!
