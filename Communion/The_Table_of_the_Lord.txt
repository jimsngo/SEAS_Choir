The Table of the LordDana Catherine/Sarah Hart1. There is healing at the table of the Lord for the wounds of all the children he adores. In his body that is broken, the redemption that we hope in. There is healing at the table of the Lord.2. There is mercy at the table of the Lord for divided hearts that need to be restored. In the blood that he has offered he forgives his sons and daughters. There is mercy at the table of the Lord.RefrainWe are one, we are one,we are one at the table of the Lord. We are loved, we are loved. We are one at the table of the Lord.3. There is myst’ry at the table of our God in the sacrifice that love has shown to us. We receive and we remember as love binds us all together. There is myst’ry at the table of our God.4. There’s a calling at the table of the Lord to loose every chain and drop our every sword, share the freedom we’ve received, welcome others to the feast. There’s a calling at the table of the Lord.© 2022, Dana Catherine Schwarz, Sarah Hart, and Hippart Music.
