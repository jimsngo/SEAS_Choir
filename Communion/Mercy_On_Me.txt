Mercy on Me
Sarah Hart

[Refrain]
Lord, have mercy on me.
Lord, have mercy on me.
Lord, have mercy on me.

[Verse 1]
For whatever I have done,
For whatever I have failed to do,
In turning my heart away from yours:
Forgive me.

[Refrain]
Lord, have mercy on me.
Lord, have mercy on me.
Lord, have mercy on me.

[Verse 2]
Fill me with humility,
A word of contrition for my sin.
Unburden my soul with kindest grace;
Absolve me.

[Refrain]
Lord, have mercy on me.
Lord, have mercy on me.
Lord, have mercy on me.

[Verse 3]
You give me a heart that's clean;
Your pardon releases me go sing.
My spirit, redeemed by love alone;
Renew me.

[Refrain]
Lord, have mercy on me.
Lord, have mercy on me.
Lord, have mercy on me.
