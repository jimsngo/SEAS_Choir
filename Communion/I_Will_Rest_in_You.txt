I Will Rest in YouFrank Jansen/ValLimar JansenRefrainI will rest in you, O Lord;I will rest in you, O Lord.I will lay down before you my heavy burdens;I will rest in you, O Lord.1. You said, “Come unto me all you who labor,who are heavy, worn and sad.Take my yoke and learn of me,and I will give you rest.”2. You said if I walk in the way,I will always be in the light.You’ll abide in me and I in you;I’ll enjoy your endless life.3. I rest in your amazing grace,in the promise of your word.I am a branch; you’re the true vine.I am nothing without your love.BridgeI will open up my heartto receive your food from heaven.I will open up my life;I will rest in you, O Lord.© 2011, Frank Jansen and ValLimar Jansen.
