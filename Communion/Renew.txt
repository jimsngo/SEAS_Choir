Renew
Sarah Hart and Marc Byrd
Vocal arr. by Rick Modlin

[Verse 1]
Our Father, Maker of the heavens and the earth,
Author of the universe who imagined us,
God All-Seeing: into our humanity comes;
Let your kindom be with us,
Oh, let your will be done.

[Verse 2]
Lord Jesus, Son of God who sits at his right hand,
Perfect Love that became man sacrificed for us,
Pure Redeemer: into our iniquity come;
Let forgiveness sweetly fall,
Have mercy on us all.

[Refrain]
Renew your people,
Renew your promise,
Renew our hearts for you.
Renew your people,
Renew your promise,
Renew our hearts for you, Lord, renew.

[Verse 3]
Holy Spirit, Gift of risen hope, abiding peace,
Comforter who intercedes, carries every prayer,
Moving Presence, Living Breath of living God: come;
Stir the passion in our hearts,
Breathe new life into us.

[Refrain]
Renew your people,
Renew your promise,
Renew our hearts for you.
Renew your people,
Renew your promise,
Renew our hearts for you, Lord, renew.
Lord, renew.
