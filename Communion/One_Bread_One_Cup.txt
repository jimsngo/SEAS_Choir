One Bread, One Cup 
Bobby Fisher, Greg Lee,
Craig Aven and Ken Canedo

[Verse 1]
We all come before you now,
Hungry for your healing touch.
Hear we stand with open hearts,
Thirsting for your love.

You are the one
Who fills our every need.
So with one voice, we lift our hearts in unity.

[Refrain]
One bread, one cup, one body of Christ, united in love.
One bread, one cup, one living sacrifice poured out in your blood.
Holy Spirit, renew us,
That in each other we may see you.
Gathered as one family in love, one bread, one cup.

[Verse 2]
As we gather at your table,
Broken like the bread we share,
Fill our hearts with your compassion
For a world in need of care.
You are the way. You are the life. You are the truth.
The source of our salvation, we find new life in you. 

[Refrain]
One bread, one cup, one body of Christ, united in love.
One bread, one cup, one living sacrifice poured out in your blood.
Holy Spirit, renew us,
That in each other we may see you.
Gathered as one family in love, one bread, one cup.

[Bridge]
When we go forth from this place of perfect peace,
May your light shine in our lives 
For all the world to see.

[Final Refrain]
One bread, one cup, one body of Christ, united in love.
One bread, one cup, one living sacrifice poured out in your blood.
Holy Spirit, renew us,
That in each other we may see you.
Gathered as one family in love, one bread, one cup.
