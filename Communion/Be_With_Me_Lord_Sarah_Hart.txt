Be with me, Lord
Sarah Hart and Curtis Stephan

[Refrain]
Be with me, Lord, when I am in trouble.
Be with me, Lord, be with me.

[Verse 1]
You who rest in the shadow of the Lord and abide in the presence of God,
Say with your heart, "My shelter and strength, my God, in you I trust."

[Refrain]
Be with me, Lord, when I am in trouble.
Be with me, Lord, be with me.

[Verse 2]
Evil will not fall upon you;
No affliction will find your door.
For the angels have been sent to protect you,
And guard you in all your ways.

[Refrain]
Be with me, Lord, when I am in trouble.
Be with me, Lord, be with me.

[Verse 3]
If you stumble they will catch you;
They will keep you from falling down.
From the den of the lion and the threat of the snake,
You will escape unharmed..

[Refrain]
Be with me, Lord, when I am in trouble.
Be with me, Lord, be with me.

[Verse 4]
Call and I will answer, says the Lord,
Trust and know I am here.
Cling to me with your life:
There is nothing you should fear.

[Refrain]
Be with me, Lord, when I am in trouble.
Be with me, Lord, be with me.
