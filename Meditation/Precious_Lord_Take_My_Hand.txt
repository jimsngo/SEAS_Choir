Precious Lord, Take My HandPRECIOUS LORD/Thomas A. Dorsey1. Precious Lord, take my hand,Lead me on, let me stand;I am tired, I am weak, I am worn;Through the storm, through the night,Lead me on to the light:Take my hand, precious Lord, lead me home.2. When my way grows drear,Precious Lord, linger near,When my life is almost gone;Hear my cry, hear my call,Hold my hand lest I fall:Take my hand, precious Lord, lead me home.3. When the darkness appearsAnd the night draws near,And the day is past and gone,At the river I stand,Guide my feet, hold my hand:Take my hand, precious Lord, lead me home.Text and music by Thomas A.
