Come, Lord! Maranatha
Ricky Manalo CSP

Advent Week 1
1. Now is the time to wake from sleep;
salvation is close at hand.
Moving from night into day,
lift up your voices and pray:

Refrain (Weeks 1–3)
Come, Lord! Maranatha!
Come and bring us your peace.
Come, Lord, Maranatha!
Come to us; set us free.
Come, Lord, and bring us your peace.

2. Cast off the deeds of the dark.
Put on the armor of light.
Turn to the source of the day.
Lift up your voices and pray:

3. Constantly watch, stay awake.
No one can know the time.
Whether at dusk or at dawn,
we sing this prayer and song:

4. Increase in faith; abound in love.
Strengthen our hearts this day.
So be on your guard and stay awake.
Come, lift up your voices and pray:

Advent Week 2
1. Now is the time; prepare the way;
salvation is close at hand.
Make straight the paths of the Lord.
Lift up your voices and pray:

Refrain (Weeks 1–3)
Come, Lord! Maranatha!
Come and bring us your peace.
Come, Lord, Maranatha!
Come to us; set us free.
Come, Lord, and bring us your peace.

2. Now is the time; reform your lives.
The reign of God is at hand!
On that day a shoot shall sprout,
blossoming; so let us pray:

3. Comfort, O God, grant to us.
Tenderly speak your Word.
Then shall your glory be revealed;
help us to cry out with zeal:

4. Take off the robe of mourning;
put on the splendor of God.
Wrap on the cloak of justice and light,
guiding us all through the night.

Advent Week 3
1. Come and rejoice, again, rejoice!
Here is the God who saves.
Now is the time, do not fear.
Sing out with voices and praise:

Refrain (Weeks 1–3)
Come, Lord! Maranatha!
Come and bring us your peace.
Come, Lord, Maranatha!
Come to us; set us free.
Come, Lord, and bring us your peace.

2. Patience, my friends; wait and see.
Soon will the Lord appear.
Patience, my friends, from the start.
Let us rejoice from our hearts:

3. Always rejoice; never cease
constantly giving thanks.
Always rejoice in the Lord.
Joyfully sing out this song:

4. Shout out with joy, O Israel.
Exult in your hearts, be glad.
The Lord, your God, is with you now;
cry out with gladness and song:

Advent Week 4
1. Come to us, O Emmanuel!
Come ransom Israel
that mourns in lonely exile here.
The Son of God soon will appear!

Refrain (Week 4)
Come, now, Emmanuel!
Come and bring us your peace.
Come, now, Emmanuel!
Come to us; set us free.
Come now and bring us your peace.

2. Come now, O Wisdom, from on high;
show us our failures and flaws.
Come to us now, O Lord of might!
Come to us; bring us your law!

3. Come now, O Rod of Jesse's stem;
power is yours alone.
Come now, O Key of David, come.
Open your heavenly home! 

4. Come now, O Dayspring from on high.
Make all the dark clouds decrease.
Come to us, O Desire of nations.
Be now our Prince of Peace.
