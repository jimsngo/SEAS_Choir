[title]
Jesus Christ Is Lord

[Author]
Ken Canedo

[Refrain]
Jesus Christ is Lord! Jesus Christ is Lord

[Verse 1]
He did not hold to his equality with God. For he did empty himself to be as one of us, To be as one of us.

[Verse 2]
He chose the will of God through death upon a cross; and God did raise him up, exalting him on high; Exalting him on high.

[Verse 3]
And so God gave to him a name above all names; May all in heaven and earth proclaim him now as Lord; Proclaim him now as Lord.
