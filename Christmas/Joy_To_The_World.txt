Joy to the World
ANTIOCH
Arranged by Dean Baskerville, Laurie Roberts and Tammy McMorrow

[Verse 1]
Joy to the world! the Lord is come;
Let earth receive her King;
Let ev'ry heart prepare him room,
And heav'n and nature sing,
And heav'n and nature sing,
And heav'n, and heav'n and nature sing.

[Refrain]
Joy! Let there be joy!
Let there be joy!
Let there be joy to the world!
Joy! Let there be joy!
Let there be joy!
Let there be joy to the world!

2. Joy to the world! the Savior reigns;
Let us our songs employ;
While fields and floods, rocks, hills and plains
Repeat the sounding joy,
Repeat the sounding joy,
Repeat, repeat the sounding joy.

[Refrain]
Joy! Let there be joy!
Let there be joy!
Let there be joy to the world!
Joy! Let there be joy!
Let there be joy!
Let there be joy to the world!

3. He rules the world with truth and grace,
And makes the nations prove
The glories of his righteousness,
And wonders of his love,
And wonders of his love,
And wonders, wonders of his love.

[Refrain]
Joy! Let there be joy!
Let there be joy!
Let there be joy to the world!
Joy! Let there be joy!
Let there be joy!
Let there be joy to the world!

Text: CM with repeats; based on Psalm 98:4–9; Isaac Watts, 1674–1748, alt. 
Music: T. Hawkes' Collection of Tunes, 1833; George Frideric Handel, 1685–1759; 
adapt. by Lowell Mason, 1792–1872, alt.
