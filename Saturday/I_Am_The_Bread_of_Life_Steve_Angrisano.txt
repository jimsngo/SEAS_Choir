I Am the Bread of Life (Come and Follow Me)Steve Angrisano/Tom Booth1. I am the Bread of Life,I am the hope in night,I am the door wide open,I am the shepherd’s might,I am the truth and light,I am the way and life,I Am Who Am and I am for you:Come and follow me.2. I am bread for the world,hope for the hopeless.Come to me, and know that I’ll always be therewith my arms open wide.I Am Who Am and I am for you:Come and follow me.BridgeI give my heart to those in sorrow,I come to those who are in need;hope for today and for tomorrow,light for all who want to see.© 2015, Steve Angrisano and Tom Booth.
