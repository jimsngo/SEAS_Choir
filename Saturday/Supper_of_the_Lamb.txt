Supper of the LambDan SchutteRefrainBlest are those invited to the Supper of the Lamb.1. Let us come, the worn and weary,to the feast prepared by Christ, the Lord.

2. Let us come, the meek and mighty,
to the feast prepared by Christ, the Lord.
3. Let us come, the lost and lonely,to the feast prepared by Christ, the Lord.

4. Let us come, the frail and fallen,to the feast prepared by Christ, the Lord.

5. Let us come, the glad and grateful,
to the feast prepared by Christ, the Lord.
6. Let us come as fathers and mothersto receive the gift of Christ, the Lord.

7. Let us come as brothers and sistersto receive the hope of Christ, the Lord.

8. Let us come as humble and homelessto receive the love of Christ, the Lord.

9. Let us come as wanting and waiting,to receive the light of Christ, the Lord.

10. Let us come as burdened and brokento receive the peace of Christ, the Lord.

11. Let us come as friend and strangerto receive the gift of Christ, the Lord.

12. Let us come as saint and sinnerto receive the hope of Christ, the Lord.

13. Let us come as sons and daughtersto receive the love of Christ, the Lord.

14. Let us come as prince and pilgrimto receive the light of Christ, the Lord.15. Let us come as young and seasonedto receive the peace of Christ, the Lord.© 2016, Daniel L. Schutte. Published by OCP. All rights reserved.
