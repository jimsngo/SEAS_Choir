Holy, Holy, Holy Cry
Rick Modlin

[Verse 1]
The Lamb of God stands on the height
Among the glorious clouds of light,
Above the city paved in gold
Where death and darkness have no hold.

[Verse 2]
In bitter sacrifice once slain,
He lives in triumph there to reign
Among the saints clad all in white
In realms where day yields not to night.

[Chorus]
"Holy, holy, holy" cry
To you our Lord Most High.
"Holy, holy, holy" cry
To you our God Most High.

[Verse 3]
To him now let our prayers arise
In clouds of incense to the skies,
From censer borne by angel hands,
Bright tongues of fire from far-flung lands.

[Verse 4]
While heavens praises hail his worth,
He catches up the prayers of earth
Unwounded hand, till, countless throng,
The singers come to join the song.

[Chorus]
"Holy, holy, holy" cry
To you our Lord Most High.
"Holy, holy, holy" cry
To you our God Most High.

[Bridge]
To Father, Son, and Spirit, Three
In One all holy Mystery.
In One all holy Mystery.

[Chorus]
"Holy, holy, holy" cry
To you our Lord Most High.
"Holy, holy, holy" cry
To you our God Most High.
