You Are The Light
Josh Blakesley

[Verse 1]
Light of the world in the cover of the night.
Glorious star in the shadows you are shining.
Hope for the lost, salvation for the sinner.
You conquered the cross with the brightness of your rising!

[Refrain]
You are the light. You are the light.
All of Heaven and Earth are singing,
You are the light.

[Verse 2]
Nations will rise to the Glory of the Father.
Kingdoms will bow to the Lamb wounded and scarred.
Your people cry out in the cities and the fields
Your people will shout, “Lord, let your glory be revealed!”

[Refrain]
You are the light. You are the light.
All of Heaven and Earth are singing,
You are the light.

[Bridge]
Tear the veil and rend the heavens!
Let the darkness burn away!

[Refrain]
You are the light. You are the light.
All of Heaven and Earth are singing,
You are the light.
