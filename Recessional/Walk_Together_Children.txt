Walk Together Children
Traditional African American Spiritual
Adapt. by ValLimar and Frank Jansen

[Verse 1]
Walk together, children, don't you get weary.
Walk together, children, don't you get weary.
Walk together, children, don't you get weary.
We will walk for justice in this great land.

[Verse 2]
Sing together, children, don't you get weary.
Sing together, children, don't you get weary.
Sing together, children, don't you get weary.
We will sing for justice in this great land.

[Refrain]
We will walk and never tire.
We will walk and never tire.
We will walk and never tire.
We will walk for justice in this great land.

[Verse 3]
Work together, children, don't you get weary.
Work together, children, don't you get weary.
Work together, children, don't you get weary.
We will work for justice in this great land.

[Refrain]
We will walk and never tire.
We will walk and never tire.
We will walk and never tire.
We will walk for justice in this great land.

[Bridge]
When everyone wins, there is true victory.
We shall overcome walking hand in hand.
Justice for all brings a freedom we seek.
We will never get tired, never get weary.
