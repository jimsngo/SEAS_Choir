Go Out, Go Out
Curtis Stephan

Refrain
Go out, go out to all the world
and tell the Good News,
tell the Good, Good News.

1. Praise the LORD,
praise the LORD, all you nations!
Glorify him,
glorify him, you peoples!

2. Steadfast,
steadfast is his kindness toward us,
and the faithfulness
of the LORD endures!
