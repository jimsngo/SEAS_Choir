God is with us
Dana Catherine, Sarah Hart,
Chris Muglia, and Mary Kate Westrich
Arr. by Rick Modlin

[Verse 1]
Go out with love, go out to serve,
Go out and tell the world that God is with us.
Carry your joy to all the earth,
Go out and tell the3 world that God is with us.

[Refrain]
We go rejoicing, knowing we are not alone;
Love has found a home in us.
We go believing in the promise of our God,
Singing Alleluia, God is with us!

[Verse 2]
Go out with hope, with mercy's hands;
Go out and show the world the God is with us.
To feed the sheep and tend the lambs,
Go out and show the world that God is with us.

[Refrain]
We go rejoicing, knowing we are not alone;
Love has found a home in us.
We go believing in the promise of our God,
Singing Alleluia, God is with us!

[Verse 3]
Go out with grace, be not afraid,
Go out and love the world, for God is with us.
Live in the light, the peace of Christ,
Go out and love the world, for God is with us.

[Refrain]
We go rejoicing, knowing we are not alone;
Love has found a home in us.
We go believing in the promise of our God,
Singing Alleluia, God is with us!
