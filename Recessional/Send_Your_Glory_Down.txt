Send Your Glory Down
Trevor Thomson
Vocal arr. by Scott Soper

[Verse 1]
O Radiant Light Devine, 
Shine within this heart of mine;
Word of God, dispel our darkness.
O Image of the Light Sublime, our God Incarnate beyond time,
That fills this place with heaven's glory:

[Refrain]
Send your glory down,
Send your glory down,
Send your glory down!

[Verse 2]
Lord Jesus Christ, as daylight fades,
We give you thanks for all our days, the work, the toil, the pain, the glory.
We praise the Father with the Son,
The Spirit blest and with them one,
We stand united as your family.

[Refrain]
Send your glory down,
Send your glory down,
Send your glory down!

[Verse 3]
O Son of God, the source of life,
We give you praise both day and night,
For you are worthy and most holy.
Unsullied lips must raise the strain of your proclaimed and splendid name:
The Lamb of God, the King of Mercy.
